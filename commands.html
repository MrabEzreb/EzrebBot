Yomamma Jokes

Use the command !yomamma to make the bot say a randomized yo mamma joke, courtesy of yomamma.info. Yo mamma so fat, the recursive function calculating her mass causes a stack overflow! xD

DnD Dice

Use the command !roll to roll DnD dice! The usage is !roll formula, where formula is <rolls>d<sides>, and after that you can add +/- another formula or even just plain numbers! Something like !roll 1d20+1d100-3d8
